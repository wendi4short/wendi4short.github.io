Dream

"Hold your horses. I know you see the little ‘Supermarket’ on the undercarriage of that title and you are thinking to yourself: “Good heavens. It appears as though our humble narrator has completely lost the plot! Supermarkets aren’t for pizza! They’re for groceries!”, and other nonsensical things that you bottom feeders think. Well I am pleased to inform you that if you were one of such lucky thinkers, you would indeed be right. And I would be wrong. "

"Just kidding. How disgusting that you – even for a second – believed that was a possibility. "

"Multiker is a not so secret Lagos spot for people that think they are really in the inner circle. But not for its goods and services. Oh no, no, no! In fact, I am pretty sure they are running a Oh wow. Look at Mr (or Mrs.) Hackerperson over here. Trying to do their little hacker things. I’m sure you feel real proud of yourself huh. You really thought you had uncovered something juicy. Epstein Files unredacting ass, Rosetta Stone translating ass, Enigma Machine deciphering ass punk. Well done though. At least you had the ingenuity to try. Here’s your reward:, but you didn’t hear it from me. Instead, Multiker is known for its ridiculously good *****. So it was with this treasure trove of good vibes, and high expectations, that when I saw la pizza pizza pizza on their menu, and a weathered looking stone oven built into the wall, I had no choice but to ‘Dive on in’. "

"Now. What do I think? Hmm. That’s a toughie. I know that’s why youre here, you greedy fucks. But that’s not particularly important. I think its far more important to talk about vibes. There is something quite comforting about having people yell over your food while its being made. Something so… A U T H E N T I C. Requests for gratis slices from regulars, queue jumping from workers, who deem themselves far too good for the MASSIVE lines that are forming just in front of the service point, quick favours that take your attendant’s attention away from your order, steaming hot pies being brought out for the display shelf just in time for the last previous slice to have been eaten, with arguments in an unknown language being unwittingly foisted upon you to settle. This place had none of that. "

"Well, that’s not entirely true. It does, but for the *****. The picture I just painted for you was not false. It was just… misattributed. The reality was, all that commotion is happening just beside you, while the actual pizza side is pretty nonplussed. Good sign? No. But hey, they do good stuff. Quality begets quality, no? "

"Welllllllll… kinda? The pie was… uninspired. Don’t get me wrong. It wasn’t bad or anything. Just uninspired. In fact, I believe that leans more towards the negative than I would like, so while I still retain the sentiment, I would instead categorise it as being inspired after all… but by a very uninspired source. Think a Chuck E. Cheese or Buffet or Cruise Ships type beat. It’s a very cheesy, very sweetly sauced, brightly doughed, very uncontroversial slice. For context it’s a bite (flavour wise, that is. Not so sure they’d vibe with the texture) that an autistic American 9-year-old that only eats chicken nuggets and other foods beginning with the letter ‘c’ would have no problem with. And yes, before you smart asses make a comment, I am well aware pizza doesn’t start with ‘c’. But Cheese pizza does. So there. It’s a very eatable, moreish, but otherwise uninteresting slice."

"And you’d best believe that your humnle narrator would chow down on that shit any day. This is why I wanted to make it clear that uninspired bad. It’s a slice that you would never go out of your way to get, but if it’s there, you wouldn’t mind indulging if you have some space in your tummy tum tum. And this distinction, this oh so secret je ne sais quoi that elevates it beyond its milquetoast contemporaries (albeit slightly) boils (hehe) down to that stone oven I made reference to earlier; Chekov’s Masonry, anyone? The crispy, slightly charred undercarriage adds that extra bit of texture, that extra bit of crunch, that extra bit of smoke, that just pushes the pie over the edge, and strait into passable territory."

"Now it goes without saying that this sort of pie, char and flour notwithstanding, can ONLY work if it’s cheap (well, not work like the item itself morphs, moreso the perception you have of it. I can accept uninspired if it costs nothing to access. That’s a well-established trade-off). And cheap it is."

"So would I be going back? Well, yes. If I am in the area, I am feeling particularly hungry or cravey, and of course, if a have a few spare thousands in my pocket, I most definitely would pay this fine establishment another visit. But I most definitely would not make a trip specifically for it. So I believe we ought to stand up together and give this one a “Smile Nigeria, say Mul-Ti-Ker!” out of C, which I believe is just dandy. "
